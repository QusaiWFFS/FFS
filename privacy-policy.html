Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 01, 2025

Last Updated: May 01, 2025

Flash Funding solutions LLC DBA Flash Financial Solutions ("Company", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information through our services, including our website, affiliate networks, phone calls, and other communication methods.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide
  • Full name
  • Contact details (email, phone number, address)
  • Date of birth
  • Social Security Number (if required for service)
  • Employment and income information
  • Debt, loan, or credit details
  • Bank account or financial information (if needed for results-based services)
B. Automatically Collected Information
  • Device type, IP address, browser type
  • Website behavior (pages visited, time spent, referring URLs)
  • Location data (if enabled on your device or browser)
C. Information from Third Parties
  • Credit reports or scores (with your consent)
  • Data from affiliates or lead sources
  • Public records and identity verification databases

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver debt relief, consolidation, or lending-related services
  • Match you with lending or affiliate partners
  • Assess your eligibility for financial solutions
  • Communicate with you via phone, SMS, or email
  • Fulfill legal and compliance requirements
  • Perform analytics, improve performance, and manage quality control
  • Prevent fraud and monitor security risks

3. How We Share Information

We never sell your data. We only share information under these conditions:

A. Third-Party Partners
  • Lending partners, debt relief providers, and credit counselors
  • Technology platforms (CRM, SMS/email services, cloud providers)
  • Credit reporting agencies (if applicable)
B. Affiliates and Lead Sources
  • If you are referred to us by a partner or affiliate, or we refer you to one
  • Only minimal personal information is shared unless you opt-in
C. Legal Requirements
  • Courts, government agencies, law enforcement, or auditors, when legally required

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical, administrative, and physical safeguards, including:

  • Data encryption
  • Firewalls and secure hosting environments
  • Access control and employee training

While we strive to protect your information, no system is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as needed:

  • To fulfill the purposes described above
  • To comply with legal, accounting, or regulatory obligations

6. Your Privacy Rights

A. General Rights (All Users)

You may:

  • Request a copy of your personal information
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Opt out of marketing communications

Contact us at: Info@flashFinancaialsolutions.com

7. CCPA Notice – California Residents Only

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grants you the following rights:

A. Your Rights Under CCPA
  • Right to Know – You can request details about what personal information we collect, use, share, or sell (we do not sell personal data).
  • Right to Delete – You can request deletion of your personal information, subject to exemptions.
  • Right to Opt Out of Sale – We do not sell personal data, but you can opt out of information sharing with third parties.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination – You will not be discriminated against for exercising your rights.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal data from anyone under 18. If we discover that a minor has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted here, with the "Last Updated" date updated accordingly. You are encouraged to check this page regularly.
